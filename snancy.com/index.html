Welcome! I'm a junior studying computer science at the California Institute of Technology (Caltech). My domain name, sNancy, is what sometimes Techers call me. As a computer programmer, my favorite language is Python, since it is a simple and clean language to read and understand.   • Jet Propulsion Laboratory under Eric de Jong as a summer undergraduate research fellow

  • Keck Institute for Space Studies as a website assistant.
For more info on my academics and work experience, check out my LinkedIn profile or resume. You can also look at some of my coding projects here!

I'm always up for freelancing opportunities in coding, art, and design. To contact me, please e-mail me at ncao@caltech.edu.
